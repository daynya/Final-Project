Hello!

I am a graphic designer and artist, currently living in Seattle, Washington. I have extensive experience in the print industry, and am expanding my repertoire with web design as well. I specialize in minimalist designs and typographical treatments. Limited color palettes, heaps of white space, elegant typestyles: these are my friends.

When I'm not sitting in front of the computer, learning how to make pretty things, I love to paint, and am currently on an outer space kick with my subject matter. I have been a lifelong creator of art, and dabble in different mediums when I can. I have taken art instruction recently at Pratt Institute in Seattle, as well as Braitman Studio in Charlotte, North Carolina.

Other bits about me: I live with my husband and our three cats. I like to cook, read, learn new tricks, and keep my body moving to offset the hours logged in a chair every day. I'm an avid lover of vegetables (to eat) and animals (to befriend). I'd love to hear from you, please reach out and say "Hi!".

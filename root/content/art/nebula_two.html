Nebula, #2

Oil on canvas

36" x 48"

The second in my ongoing Nebula Series. I stepped up the scale on this piece. The subject matter lends itself well to large canvases. This is an effort to connect, on a deep level, to the stardust contained within us all. The mystery and beauty that the nebula offers mirrors the depths of the same contained within us, as humans living in a universe of star matter.

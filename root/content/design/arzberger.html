Arzberger

Development of online catalog

Arzberger has been handcrafting custom stationery for nearly 100 years. In an effort to modernize the interface through which they offer this product, I developed an online catalog showcasing the categories, products, and information needed to place orders. The main task here was making it easy to use. The demographics vary widely, but there is a large portion of older users who are reluctant to move to an online system. The eventual goal with this project is moving to an online ordering portal.

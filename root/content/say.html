Strong Ass Yoga - in progress

Development of yoga website

This is a website I am working on for a side project of mine. In another life I was a yoga instructor. In yet another one, I was a competitive powerlifter. In this life, I realized that these two things benefit each other in a unique way and have decided to offer a program that helps bring the wonderful effects of yoga to the world of the gym. I'm developing content to populate this site with a yoga program, photos, videos, and downloadable guides. As that content gets generated, I will update that project, and update it's progress here as well.

For the build, I usedSignal, and Bootstrap. The main focus is going to be the content.

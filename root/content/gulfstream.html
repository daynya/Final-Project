Gulf Stream Website

Click here to visit the Gulf Stream site.

Development of website for Gulf Stream Asset Management

Gulf Stream contacted me with need of a clean and elegant brochure site to inform clients of the company history and reputation. The beautiful photography and minimal text keep the site on track without being distracting.

They wanted to incorporate a video into the homepage, which we accomplished using a Vimeo subscription. This made it easy to to embed their video and customize it to their specifications. The site includes a history of the company, as well as the people who run it. It includes contact info, company values, and a secure client login page to access sensitive documents.

As I do with all recent projects, I built this site using Signal for the templating engine. I also used Bootstrap for the grid here. They needed many modals included for the bios of their team, and Boostrap offers an easy tool for this task.

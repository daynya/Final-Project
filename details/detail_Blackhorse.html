Blackhorse Coffeehouse

Business card and menu design
Blackhorse Coffee is a small, artisinal coffee shop that is obsessed with coffee. They are extremely picky about their sources, and so I did not want to detract from that in the design. The card shows a coffee stain, because that is the most important aspect of this business. The menu is no frills, lots of white space, drawing the eye to the information that is presented.

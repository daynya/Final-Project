Nebula, #2

Oil on canvas
36" x 48"
Inspired by space, and the infinity that surrounds us, this painting was my attempt at capturing some of the beauty of the mysterious nebula. I used many thin layers to achieve the dreamy, cloud like particles that you see on this canvas. This painting took me about 6 months to complete, comprised of many baby steps. You can also see here my quick color study that I did in the beginning stages of planning this large piece.
